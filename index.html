üí° Pro Strategic Tips

  • Momentum is Key: Your ship has inertia. Don't wait until the last second to change direction. Anticipate the asteroid and missile paths.
  • Red Alert: Red missiles are heat-seeking. They will follow you! Make sharp, sudden turns to break their tracking lock or lead them into asteroids.
  • Shield Management: Collecting hearts repairs your hull. Don't risk a collision for a heart if your health is full, but prioritize them when low.
  • Zone Awareness: The center of the screen is safest, but also the most crowded. The edges offer escape routes but limit your maneuverability.
