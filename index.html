Space One: Interstellar Survival

Welcome to Space One, an infinite arcade space survival game. Pilot the last MK-IV Interceptor through the quarantine sector.

Game Instructions

  • Move: Use Arrow Keys (Desktop) or Drag Joystick (Mobile) to navigate.
  • Dodge: Avoid colliding with asteroids and planets.
  • Survive: Red missiles track your heat signature. Make sharp turns to evade them.
  • Repair: Collect hearts to repair hull damage.

Lore

The Universe is infinite... unfortunately so are the enemies. Sector 7G quarantine has been lifted. Engines are online. How long can you survive the void?
